Arjuna Drona
The term "Geeta" likely refers to the Bhagavad Gita, which is a sacred Hindu scripture. The Bhagavad Gita is a 700-verse dialogue that is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata. It is a conversation between Prince Arjuna and Lord Krishna, who serves as his charioteer. The conversation takes place on the battlefield of Kurukshetra just before a great war is about to begin.
